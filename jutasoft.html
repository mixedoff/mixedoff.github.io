Remastering a classic DOS cashier app's UI.

Summary

The point of the project was to redesign a classic DOS experience made for shop inventories. It was not meant to be a fully featured program, just a trial version, so I recreated a possible main flow and with that the style and feeling, micro interactions and design system.


Inspiration

I went to a shop nearby and the DOS program they've used for inventorying really caught my attention. Even though it had very little design (they went all out on usability) the style was so retro, that it looked very unique. I decided to redesign the program but try to keep the design as close to the original as possible. With that said I have also incorporated some design from one of my favourite DOS game: Keen Commander.


Styles

One of the core tenets of the program's design was the classic navy blue background (apart from it's simplicity). So I've reintroduced a subtle blue circle pattern as a background to spice things up a little bit...
